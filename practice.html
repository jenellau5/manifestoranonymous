What does this feel most like right now?

Why this exists

Manifestor anger builds when your energy has nowhere to go.

When you keep swallowing what you really want to say, let people manage your energy, push past your real pace, or stay too long in what already feels wrong, anger starts stacking.

The Rage Room gives that anger somewhere to land so you can see what it is actually protecting instead of turning it on yourself.

Use this when
  • You feel like everyone is in your way and you want to disappear or explode.
  • You keep getting interrupted, questioned, or controlled.
  • You are exhausted but still forcing yourself to keep going.
  • You are tired of being the calm one when you are not calm.
The anger patterns in this room
Control anger

When your energy is being managed, questioned, or slowed down too much.

Voice anger

When your words get swallowed, ignored, or talked over.

Repressed anger

When you are holding too much in and resentment has started building underneath.

Exhaustion anger

When you have pushed past your real energy and your body is already done.

Self-betrayal anger

When you already knew something was wrong and stayed in it anyway.
