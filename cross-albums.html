Custom Incarnation Cross Playlist (Life Theme).

Deluxe edition · 4‑gate playlist

This is where your chart stops being a PDF and starts sounding like something. You send the four gates of your incarnation cross and 1–5 artists or genres your system actually likes. You get a custom 20–30 track playlist (about 1.5–2 hours of music) built around those four gates, curated only from the artists/genres you chose, plus a simple PDF cover with your cross name and a QR code to your playlist.

Inside the PDF you get a mini life‑theme reading: a breakdown of your cross, a short write‑up for each gate, and an overview of why each song was paired with that gate. It’s not a long written reading to scroll. It’s your life theme translated into four gates and a soundtrack you can actually play.
