Too Much Room.

The contract that taught you to become smaller than your actual power.

This is the room where Manifestors learn that their directness, pace, scale, aura, standards, or certainty need to be toned down to stay acceptable.

Your job in this room is not to become louder for performance. Your job is to expose the contract, revoke it, replace it, and stop paying for other people’s comfort with your energy.

You manage other people’s emotions

You delay the thing you actually want to do

Underpricing to stay likable

Explaining until it became permission

Before: A Manifestor leader explained every decision until the room calmed down.

Turning point: They realized explanation had become emotional labor for everyone else.

Carrying the emotional room
